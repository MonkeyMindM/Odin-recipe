Honey_GLazed_Chicken Recipe "Easy"


Today we will be learnig a nice, simple, and delicious recipe. Some delicious
Sweet Honey Glazed Chicken with rice. Lets Begin!

Ingridients

  • ¼ cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • ⅛ teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 ½ tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into bite-size pieces

Directions


  1. Gather all Ingridients
  2. Whisk honey, soy sauce, and red pepper flakes in a bowl; set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir chicken in hot
    oil until lightly brown, about 5 minutes.
  4. Pour honey mixture into the skillet; continue to cook and stir until chicken is
    no longer pink in the center and sauce is thickened, about 5 minutes more.
  5. Pour honey mixture into the skillet; continue to cook and stir until chicken is
    no longer pink in the center and sauce is thickened, about 5 minutes more.
